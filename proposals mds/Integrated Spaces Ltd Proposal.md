documented process, it is impossible to hire someone else to do the selling. The biggest challenge right now

This 2-week engagement (The DT Blueprint) provides the "Day 1" instructions. We are building a step-by-step
guide that allows the founder to stop being the only salesperson and start being the manager of a sales

We move from "where do we start" to a "ready-to-run" plan in two phases:
● **Phase 1: Removing the Guesswork (Days 1–7)**
○ **The Target:** We define exactly who we are going after, so no time is wasted on the wrong leads.
○ **The Filter:** We create a qualification checklist to quickly identify if a prospect is worth your
time.

● **Phase 2: Creating the "Action Kit" (Days 8–14)**
○ **What to Say:** We write the scripts and messages for outreach so you aren't starting from a
blank page.

## Annexure B: The DT Research Methodology

**How we move from "Referral-Only" to a "Strategic Sales Plan."** Before we write a single script or hire a single
person, we complete four specific documentation blocks. This ensures the execution plan is grounded in the
reality of your business.

##### 1. Positioning Strategy (The RCA-CSA Framework)

We begin by analyzing where you stand today versus where you want to be.
**Root Cause Analysis (RCA):** We identify why sales are currently stuck at the referral stage and what specific
barriers prevent cold prospects from buying.
**Current State Analysis (CSA):** We document your existing win rates and the "why" behind your successful
projects.
**The Result:** A clear statement of your **Unique Value Proposition** —why a stranger should trust you over a
competitor they already know.

##### 2. ICP Definition (Customer Archetyping)

We stop the "everyone is a customer" approach. By looking at your most profitable and smoothest referral
projects, we define your **Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)**.
**Data Points:** We analyze company size, industry, geography, and "Buying Trigger" (the specific event that makes
them need you).
**The Result:** A specific "Target List" profile that guides where your sales team spends 100% of their time.

##### 3. Channel Selection (Persona Mapping)

We don't pick sales channels (Field, Phone, or Digital) based on trends; we pick them based on where your **ICP**
actually spends their time.
**Persona Analysis:** Does your buyer answer the phone? Do they visit trade shows? Do they search on Google?
**The Result: one primary channel** to master first, rather than spreading resources too thin.

##### 4. Messaging Strategy (The Funnel Talk)

We translate your technical expertise into "Buyer Language."
We map specific messages to each stage of the sales journey:
**Awareness:** What do we say to someone who has never heard of you?
**Consideration:** How do we prove your expertise compared to cheaper options?
**Decision:** What is the final "push" that converts a lead into a client?
**The Result:** A **Messaging Framework** that ensures whoever you hire sounds like a seasoned pro from day one.

##### Transition to Execution: Only after these four blocks are documented do we provide the Execution

Blueprint (Annexure A). This sequence ensures that the hiring and outreach plans are built on a foundation of
facts, not just "sales energy."
