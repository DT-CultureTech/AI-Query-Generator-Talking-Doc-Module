# Recruitment Program for Unique International

## 1. Objective

Unique International needs to hire a **Technical Sales Executive** with:
● CNC / machining knowledge (or the ability to learn fast)
● Confidence to represent the company as the first non-founder hire
● **Ownership mindset**
Previous attempts have not yielded candidates with the required clarity, mindset, or
confidence. There is also a hiring-risk concern: if the candidate fails in 4 months, the
company loses approx. ₹2 lakhs.
DeepThought (DT) will provide **consulting** , not recruitment, to reduce this risk by designing
a structured selection system, candidate learning modules, and employer branding assets.

## 2. Scope of Work

### A. Selection Process Design

DT will design a role-specific selection process that includes:

1. **Role Blueprint** – responsibilities, KPIs, expectations
2. **Shortlisting Rubric** – criteria for evaluating candidates
3. **Role Assignment / Case Task** – to test mindset, learning ability, and sales approach
4. **Structured Interview Framework** – founder-friendly interview checklist
This enables faster and more accurate evaluation.

### B. Candidate Learning Material (Pre-Interview L&D)

To ensure candidates attend interviews with relevant understanding:

1. CNC basics in simple format
2. Product + customer profile explainer
3. Sales process fundamentals
4. Founder expectations and role clarity
This especially helps candidates without prior CNC experience gain confidence.


### C. Employer Branding Support

Good candidates evaluate the company before joining. DT will prepare:

1. One-page founder introduction
2. Company profile & future direction
3. Role growth pathway
4. Job description rewritten for clarity
5. Messaging for calls, emails, and candidate conversations
This helps attract candidates who are career-serious.
